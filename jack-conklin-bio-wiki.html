Who is Jack Conklin?

Jack Conklin is an American football player. He currently plays as an offensive tackle for the Tennessee Titans of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Michigan State University.

Jack Conklin’s Early life, childhood, and education

Jack was born on August 17, 1994, in Plainwell, Michigan, USA.  He is the son of Native American parents Jennifer Conklin and Darren Conklin. His father is a football coach. Jack is an American by nationality and is of Native American ethnicity.

He attended Plainwell High School in Plainwell, Michigan, where he played football under his father who was the head coach. He also played basketball in high school. After high school, he joined the Michigan State University football team as a walk-on player.

Conklin was selected as a 2015 First-Team All-American and the 2015 First-Team All-Big Ten. At that time, he was the third-highest-rated offensive tackle behind Laremy Tunsil and Ronnie Stanley.
