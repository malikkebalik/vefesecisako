Is MLB The Show 23 available on PC? All you need to know

July 05, 2024
blog

As the new baseball season dawns, players eagerly flock to MLB The Show 23, the latest addition to the MLB video game franchise. This installment boasts several exciting new features, including a Storylines game mode and updated legend cards in the Diamond Dynasty. Regarding the availability of the game on PC, below is a comprehensive rundown of all the essential details.

In 2021, baseball fans rejoiced when the game expanded beyond its original PlayStation-exclusive status to include Xbox and Nintendo Switch users. However, PC players were disappointed as they were left out of the fun.

Now, with the release of MLB The Show 23, PC players are hoping that they will finally get a chance to enjoy the game. The community eagerly anticipates the possibility of finally being able to play the beloved game on their personal computers.

Can PC users now access MLB The Show 23?

Unfortunately for PC players, MLB The Show 23 will not be available on their platform this year, forcing them to wait yet another year. Meanwhile, the game can be accessed on various other platforms, such as the Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4 and 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series S/X.

However, for those with an Xbox Game Pass Ultimate subscription, there is still an option to play MLB The Show 23 on their PC, though it is not a full port.

Early access to the game started on March 24 for players who purchased the Digital Deluxe or Captain Edition, as well as the Next Generation Edition. The Standard Edition, on the other hand, was launched on March 28, allowing all players to finally get their hands on the game.

How to play MLB The Show 23 using the Xbox PC app

If you’re interested in playing MLB The Show 23 via the Xbox PC app, simply follow these steps:

  • If you haven’t done so already, download and install the Xbox PC app.
  • Sign in with your Xbox account and ensure that you have an active subscription to Xbox Game Pass Ultimate.
  • Navigate to the Cloud Gaming tab located on the left-hand side of the screen.
    Scroll down until you find MLB The Show 23 and select it.
  • Click on the green Play button to start playing the game.

At the moment, there is no official word on whether MLB The Show 23 will be released on PC. Hence, it’s unlikely that you’ll find the game on Steam or the Epic Games Store. However, we’ll keep you updated if there are any changes.

Baseball games to play on PC as an alternative to MLB The Show 23

If you’re a PC player and are disappointed that MLB The Show 23 isn’t available on your platform, there are other baseball games that you can try instead. Here are some good alternatives to MLB The Show 23 that you can play on PC:

  • Super Mega Baseball 3: This game offers a fun and accessible baseball experience with a range of customizable options to suit your style of play.
  • Out of the Park Baseball 22: If you’re looking for a more strategic and in-depth baseball simulator, this game is a great choice. It allows you to manage a team, make roster moves, and simulate seasons.
  • Baseball Mogul: This is a simulation-based baseball management game where you take control of a team and try to build a winning dynasty.

    • While none of these games may offer the same experience as MLB The Show 23, they still provide plenty of baseball fun and excitement on your PC.
