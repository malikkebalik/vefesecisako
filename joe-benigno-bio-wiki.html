Who is Joe Benigno?

Joe Benigno is an American sports host. He is a radio host and works for a radio station WFAN, New York.

Joe is also a writer and has written a book named Rules for New York Sports Fans in 2010.

Joe Benigno: Age, Siblings, Parents, Ethnicity

He was born Joseph S. Benigno on Sep 26, 1953, in Garfield, New Jersey, USA.

His mother, father, and siblings’ names are not disclosed.

He belongs to the Italian ethnicity.

Education

Regarding his academic information, he attended Franklin College, Indiana. There he studied political science and completed his graduation in 1975.

He was also a student of Paramus High School and Connecticut School of Broadcasting – Farmington CT.

Joe Benigno: Professional Life, Career

Joe Benigno is a popular face in the entertainment industry. In 1994, he started his radio career. His first host was as a guest on WFAN.

After this, he hosted several shows with Sid Rosenberg and Evan Roberts. With Evan, he hosts a show named Joe & Evan.

It is a talk show and is aired on New York radio stations.
