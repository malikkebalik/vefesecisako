    Troy Aikman Facts

  • He spent his childhood years growing up in Cerritos, California until the age of 12 when his family shifted to Henryetta, Oklahoma.
  • In the 1989 NFL Draft, Troy Aikman was selected as the first overall pick by the Dallas Cowboys.
  • In 1999, he underwent LASIK surgery.
  • He was inducted into the Texas Sports Hall of Fame in 2001.
  • Once, Troy Aikman was named the most eligible bachelor in Dallas by Texas Monthly.
  • The trio of Troy Aikman, Emmitt Smith, and Michael Irvin was nicknamed the “Triplets” of the 1990s and they were elected to the Dallas Cowboys‘ Ring of Honor in September 2005.
  • In February 2006, he was elected to the Pro Football Hall of Fame.
  • Troy Aikman was inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame in 2008.
  • Along with Nolan Ryan, Emmitt Smith, Tom Landry, and Roger Staubach, Troy Aikman was inducted into the ESPN Dallas Hall of Fame in 2011 (inaugural class).
