Jim Jordan Wikipedia

Jim Jordan Wikipedia: - Jordan, the son of Shirley and John Jordan, was born on February 17, 1964, in Troy, Ohio, and reared in Champaign County, Ohio. He went to Graham High School and wrestled for them, graduating in 1982. He won state titles in four years of high school and had a 156-1 win-loss record. He subsequently attended the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where he won two NCAA Division I wrestling championships. Jordan holds a master's degree in teaching from Ohio State University and a Juris Doctorate from Capital University Law School. Jordan stated in a 2018 interview that he never took the bar test.
