Who is Olivia Holt?

People who utilize their talents are an inspiration to not only everyday individuals, but they send a positive message in the world as well. And today we are going to talk about Olivia Holt, a 22-year-old American actress, and singer. She is a famous actress whose beauty and acting has attract many individuals.

Olivia appeared in the Disney XD series Kickin' It, Disney Channel Original Movie Girl vs. Monster, and the Disney Channel Original Series I Didn't Do It. Likewise, her debut EP Olivia was released on Hollywood Records on July 15, 2016. Some of the child actors who gained fame with Disney roles are Paris Berelc and Ryan Ochoa. Let's know in full detail about the lovely Olivia Holt.

Holt was born as Olivia Hastings Holt on August 5, 1997, in Germantown, Tennessee, the U.S. Her parents are Mark (father) and Kim Holt (mother). When she was three, her family moved to Nesbit, Mississippi where Olivia grew up with her siblings Cade Holt (brother) and Morgan Toll Holt (sister). As of 2019, Holt is 22 years old, and her birth sign is Leo.

In 2011, Holt moved to Los Angeles with her family. She began acting in local theater productions and appeared in television commercials at the age of 10. Some of her early credits include Hasbro, Kidz Bop 14, Mattel, Bratz dolls, Littlest Pet Shop, and Girl Gourmet.

Holt was cast in the Disney XD's martial arts comedy Kickin' It as she had gymnastic skills. In January 2017, she was cast as Tandy Bowen/Dagger, one of the lead characters in the Freeform and Marvel Comics tv series Marvel's Cloak & Dagger.

Her notable works in the films include Class Rank, Same Kind of Different as Me, Status Update, and Gnome Alone. Talking about her musical career, Holt recorded three songs for the movie Girl vs. Monster.

She won a 2013 Radio Disney Music Award for Best Crush Song for "Had Me @ Hello", which was featured in the film. In April 2017, she was picked as Elvis Duran's Artist of the Month appearing on NBC's Today show. Likewise, she also won the 2018 Teen Choice Awards for the tv series, Cloak & Dagger.
