The Indian Hindi web series Kota Factory was made by Saurabh Khanna, who also directed it. Arunabh Kumar was in charge of making it. Saurabh Khanna and Raghav Sabbu made and directed the Indian Hindi-language web series Kota Factory for The Viral Fever.

The action takes place in Kota, Rajasthan, which is a well-known center for education with many places to learn. The third season of Kota Factory is set to come out on August 26, 2022. The story takes place in Kota, Rajasthan, which is a popular place for education because of its coaching centers. The main character of the play is Vaibhav, a 16-year-old from Itarsi played by Mayur More.

It’s about city students and Vaibhav’s efforts to get into an Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) through the Joint Entrance Examination. The amazing Kota Factory Season 3 cast is made up of Jitendra Kumar, Ahsaas Channa, Alam Khan, Ranjan Raj, Revathi Pillai, and Urvi Singh. All of them play important roles.

Storyline Of Kota Factory Season 3

The idea for Kota Factory came from the experiences of students at a coaching center in Kota, India, who are getting ready to take the IIT entrance exam. The basic plot of this movie is similar to that of the 2009 movie “3 Idiots,” the online show “Laakhon Mein Ek,” and the documentary drama “An Engineer’s Dream.”

Tanul Thakur of the British magazine The Wire, on the other hand, says that the series is more realistic than movies with similar plots. Thakur says that the first half of the series is worth it because of “intellectual curiosity about the city and its people.”

He went on to say that the show didn’t just show things as they really are, but also made it okay for coaches to have quirks instead of criticizing the Coaching Institute. A big part of the show is also about how hard it is to be a student in Kota.
