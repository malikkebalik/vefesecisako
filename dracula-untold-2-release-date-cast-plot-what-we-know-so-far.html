What was the name of the first vampire?

Jure Grando Alilovi (1579-1656) was a villager from the province of Istria (in modern-day Croatia) who may have been the first true person documented in historical documents as a vampire. He was known as a strigoi, trigon, or Trigun, a local term for anything similar to a vampire or a warlock.
