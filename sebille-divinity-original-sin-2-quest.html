    Starting Map: Fort Joy

    Quest will be added to your journal after Sebille joins the team.

    Sebille joined my party. It seems she very adamantly wants to question a certain lizard. She -and her needle- are clearly not to be trifled with. Makes me wonder what'll happen when we find the one she seeks.

    Quest stages of Sebille

    1. Stingtail

    Find Stingtail and let Sebille talk to him.

    Warning: Sebille will kill Stingtail! So if you want to talk to him do it first.

    Sebille tortured and killed Stingtail, a lizard Dreamer. Before dying he told her only her Master can remove the scar she seeks to be rid of. Her next clue is to look into a mercenary band called the Lone Wolves. The local cook, Griff, should know more.

    2. Griff

    Go to Camp Kitchen and let Sebille talk to Camp Boss Griff.

    Sebille met with Griff and learned that there's a Lone Wolf smuggler out in the swamps known as the Hollow Marshes. Zaleskar is the smuggler's name. If she manages to find him, that'll be her chance to question a Lone Wolf.

    3. Saheila Met

    Sebille met with a blind elf named Saheila, who recognised something in her, but wouldn't say what. She instead said that Sebille will have to discover it for herself.

    4. Zaleskar

    Find Zaleskar and let Sebille talk to him.

    Sebille learned that it was a Lone Wolf called Roost who sold her into slavery to the Master years ago. If anyone knows where the Master is, it is him. She must travel to Reaper's Coast and track the bastard down.

    5. Start LV

    This case will occur if Sebille joined your team for the first time aboard Lady Vengeance.

    Sebille joined the party. She's looking for her Master, who once enslaved her. She intends to kill him. To find this master, she must seek a Lone Wolf by the name of Roost Anlon, as it was he who first kidnapped her and sold her to her Master.

    6. LV Scar

    The Lady Vengeance told us that it was controlled by a slave scar - the same kind of scar that Sebille has.

    7. LV Mother Tree

    The Lady Vengeance told us that the Mother Tree intends to spread her roots throughout the entire world and make it her own.

    8. LV Unselected Close

    If Sebille will not be in your party while fighting Dallis on Lady Vengeance, she will die and you will no longer be able to invite her.

    Sebille died on the Lady Vengeance as we tried to escape Dallis' attack.

    9. Chapter 4 Start

    Sebille knows that Roost Anlon was seen somewhere in the region. She seeks the Lone Wolf.

    10. Chapter 4 Start (Lone Wolf)

    Sebille believes that there are Lone Wolves somewhere in the region. They may be able to help her find her old Master.

    11. Chapter 4 Start (No Roost)

    Landing on the shores of Reaper's Coast jogs Sebille's memory. The Lone Wolves who kidnapped her and sold her to the Master may well be somewhere in the region. If she can find them, she may move one step closer to finding - and confronting - her old Master.

    12. Baran

    Baran Levere.

    Sebille learned that Roost Anlon may be holed up in an abandoned sawmill, somewhere outside Driftwood.

    13. Roost

    Find Roost Anlon and let Sebille talk to him.

    Sebille confronted Roost, who told her the Master is on an island with no name... Should we learn more about this location, that is where we should head.

    13.1. Roost Dead

    Roost Anlon, the man who delivered Sebille to the Master, is dead. She has only one target left: the Master himself.

    14. Elf Site Tree

    Sebille met with an Ancestor Tree that told her to find 'the Mother' so that she can heal all elves. I wonder what that's about.

    15. Saheila (Post Roost)

    Release Saheila, take her out of the sawmill and talk to her in the Elven Camp.

    Saheila revealed that Sebille is also a Scion - in fact, she is the Prime Scion. Saheila urged Sebille to travel to the Nameless Isle and find the Mother Tree.

    16. Tovah (Post Roost)

    Tovah, the elven warrior, revealed that Sebille is also a Scion - in fact, she is the Prime Scion. Tovah urged Sebille to travel to the Nameless Isle and find the Mother Tree.

    17. Voidwoken Tree

    Void-tainted Ancestor Tree.

    From the memories of a Void-tainted Ancestor tree in the Stonegarden, Sebille learned that Scions are enslaved by the Mother Tree, and forced to sacrifice themselves for the cause of the elves by taking root.

    18. TNI Start Nothing

    We landed on the shores of the Nameless Isle. We know we must tread carefully.

    19. TNI Start (Know Spy)

    We landed on the shores of the Nameless Isle - where, according to Roost Anlon, Sebille's old master may be found. We need to travel carefully.

    20. TNI Start (Know Mother)

    We landed on the shores of the Nameless Isle. The Mother Tree is rooted here. Sebille may wish to talk to her.

    21. TNI Start (Know Spy Mother)

    We landed on the shores of the Nameless Isle. Here, we are told, Sebille's quarry - her old master - may be found, and here too the Mother Tree is rooted. We have much to do, but should tread carefully.

    22. The Shadow Prince

    The Shadow Prince.

    22.1. Knows Scar Song

    Sebille trusted me enough to teach me her scar-song. An honour indeed - and more importantly, a form of insurance for her should we run into the Shadow Prince, her old master.

    22.2. Avoid Master

    Sebille does not quite trust me enough to help her should we run into the Shadow Prince. She prefers to avoid him, at least for now.

    22.3. Spymaster Possessed

    The Shadow Prince sang Sebille's scar-song, and she belongs to him once more.

    22.4. Song Countered

    The Shadow Prince sang Sebille's scar-song, but we were able to counter him. She did not lose herself to him this time.

    22.4.1. Spymaster Killed

    The Shadow Prince is dead. Sebille is free.

    23. The Mother Tree

    23.1. Mother Scion Kill Spymaster

    Talk to Elven Scion (lvl 17).

    Sebille spoke with the Mother Tree, who asked her to kill the Shadow Prince.

    23.2. Rooted

    The Mother Tree asked Sebille to take root, to become the new Mother after she dies - to be the heart of elvendom, the hope of the elves. Sebille acquiesced. She now believes she needs to become the next Divine also.

    23.3. Not Rooted

    The Mother Tree asked Sebille to take root, to become the new Mother after she dies - to be the heart of elvendom, the hope of the elves. Sebille refused.

    23.4. Mother Eat Heart

    The Scion of the Mother Tree ate the heart of the Shadow Prince, then asked Sebille to take root.

    23.5. Saheila Wants Kill

    You will meet Saheila after you leave the Heart of the Mother Tree.

    Saheila asked Sebille to liberate the elves - by killing the Mother Tree.

    23.6. Killed Mother Tree

    Sebille killed the Mother Tree.

    23.7. Killed Mother Tree Death Fog

    Sebille no longer needs to kill the Mother Tree herself: it was destroyed by Deathfog. One way or another, it's over.

    24. TNI Submitted

    Sebille wanted to ascend to divinity at the Well of Ascension. I agreed and will stand beside her.

    25. TNI Allowed

    Sebille supported me in my quest to become Divine.

    26. TNI Opposed Close

    Sebille chose to fight me for Divinity.

    27. DNOTS Not Love

    Sebille and I had a night of passion and intimacy. It was rather wonderful.

    28. TNI Face Master

    We approached the Well of Ascension, but Sebille asked that we face her former master before proceeding.

    29. DNOTS Love

    Sebille and I considered becoming lovers, but in the end we remained friends and nothing more.

    30. Chapter 6 Start

    The Shadow Prince still lives, but we must move on in search of Divinity. We can only hope that we will become powerful enough to overcome the domination of Sebille's scar.

    31. Chapter 6 Start (Spy)

    Sebille was freed of the tyranny of her master and of the Mother Tree too. But now she desires to end enslavement across the world - or all the blood spilled in the name of freedom will have been wasted. Dallis, the God King, and perhaps one of our companions, are on a quest to take over the world.

    32. Chapter 6 Start (Root Spy)

    Sebille's old master is dead, and we have taken responsibility for the whole of elvendom. Sebille shall be their new Mother. We shall require all the powers of Divinity in order to be able to protect the elves.

    33. Saheila Dryad

    Find and talk to Saheila in Arx.

    33.1. Saheila Dryad (Root)

    We encountered Saheila, in dryad form. She thanked Sebille for rooting into the new Mother Tree, and told her that she needs to seize the powers of Divinity for her people.

    33.2. Saheila Dryad (No Root)

    We encountered Saheila, in dryad form. She mourned Sebille's unwillingness to root into the new Mother Tree, and told her that she must claim the powers of Divinity for her people.

    33.3. Saheila Dryad (Dead Tree)

    We encountered Saheila, in dryad form. She reminded Sebille that while the elves are now free of the Mother Tree, she should still try to claim the powers of Divinity for them.

    34. Crypt Allowed

    Sebille supported me as I stake my claim for Divinity.

    35. Crypt Denied

    Sebille chose to fight me for Divinity.

    36. Crypt Divine

    Sebille claimed Divinity and I supported her. Sebille is the new Divine.

    37. Gen Leave Party

    Sebille chose to leave my party and journey on alone.

    38. Sebille Dead

    Sebille is dead.
