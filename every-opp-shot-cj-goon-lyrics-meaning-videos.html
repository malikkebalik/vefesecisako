Overall Meaning

The song "How You Every O Shot?" by Cj Goon is a hard-hitting trap song that talks about street violence, gang culture, and the pressure to maintain a reputation in the rap industry. The lyrics are explicit and full of bravado, with the artist calling out other rappers and gang members who he believes to be fake or weak. The chorus repeats the phrase "How you every O shot, nigga, who did you shoot?" which is a reference to the O-block neighborhood in Chicago, known for its history of gang activity and violent crime. The artist also mentions his crew, the OY-OGz, and how they are always ready to back him up in a fight or a shootout.


The verses describe the artist's mindset when he's "creepin'" or looking for a target to attack. He talks about how he's always armed with a gun or a knife, and how he doesn't hesitate to use it if he feels threatened. He also talks about his loyalty to his friends and his willingness to protect them at all costs, even if it means getting into a dangerous situation. The song ends with a shoutout to other members of the OY-OGz, and a final warning to anyone who tries to cross them.
