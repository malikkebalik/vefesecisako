What is John Schneider’s Net Worth?

John Schneider, the American actor and country singer, has a net worth of $200 thousand. He is best known for his role as Bo Duke in “The Dukes of Hazzard.”

However, Schneider has faced several legal and financial issues in recent years, which are detailed below.

Early Life and Career

John Richard Schneider was born on April 8, 1960, in Mount Kisco, New York. He is the youngest of three sons born to Shirley Conklin and John “Jack” Schneider III, who served in the U.S. Air Force as a pilot. Unfortunately, Schneider’s other brother died before his birth.

From a young age, Schneider had a passion for entertaining and began performing magic shows for friends and family at the age of eight. He was so dedicated to his acting career that he graduated from high school a year early to focus on his craft.

John Schneider’s Acting Career

John Schneider began his professional acting career at the age of seventeen when he landed the role of Bo Duke on “The Dukes of Hazzard”. He appeared in 127 episodes of the popular show, which followed the adventures of two young cousins and their friends and family members. Schneider also directed the episode “Opening Night at the Boar’s Nest”, which was the series finale.

During his time on “The Dukes of Hazzard”, Schneider also starred in several television films and lent his voice as a voice actor to a few projects. He reprised his role as Bo Duke in subsequent projects related to the show.

After “The Dukes of Hazzard”, Schneider continued to act in television films and appeared on several shows. He played Jonathan Kent, the adoptive father of Clark Kent, on “Smallville” for the first five seasons and appeared in 103 episodes. Schneider also directed some of the episodes of “Smallville”.

John Schneider’s Success Beyond Acting

Aside from his acting career, John Schneider was a country singer in the 1980s. He produced ten albums and eighteen singles, all of which reached the top spot on the Billboard Country singles chart.

In 2018, Schneider joined season 27 of “Dancing with the Stars” as a competitor. His professional partner was Emma Slater, but they were eliminated in the sixth round of the competition.

Personal Life

John Schneider has been married four times. In 1983, he married Tawny Elaine Godin, a former Miss America, and they divorced in 1986. He then married Elly Castle in 1993, with whom he has three children, including Castle’s two children from her previous marriage. Castle filed for divorce in 2014. Schneider began dating Alicia Allain in April 2015, who is not only his producing partner but also his personal and professional manager. They got married in September 2019.

John Schneider Falls Behind on Spousal Support Payments

In February 2018, it was revealed that John Schneider was more than $150,000 behind on spousal support payments to his ex-wife, Elly Castle. Schneider had been ordered to pay Castle $18,000 per month in support. To remedy the situation, he was ordered to give Castle ownership of a property he owns in Apple Valley, California.

During the court proceedings, Schneider apologized and expressed his embarrassment and humiliation to be in his financial situation. Castle complained that Schneider put himself in this position by taking “crappy” movie and TV jobs and investing in low-budget, poorly produced movies and TV shows. She also claimed that Schneider’s primary source of income is an investment property in Louisiana.

In August 2019, a judge ordered Schneider to pay Castle $25,000 per month in spousal support, a cut of his SAG pension and sole ownership of their $600,000 property.
