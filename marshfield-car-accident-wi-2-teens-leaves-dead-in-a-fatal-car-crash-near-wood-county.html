Marshfield Car Accident: In a devastating incident that unfolded in Wood County on Monday morning, two young lives were tragically lost in a two-vehicle collision. The accident occurred when Quinton Plucinski was driving southbound on Polish Road, and the vehicle he and Conner Ostricki were in failed to stop at a stop sign at the intersection. At that precise moment, a semi-truck, traveling eastbound on Highway 73, collided with their vehicle. The impact of the collision was so severe that both Plucinski and Ostricki were pronounced dead at the scene of the accident.

Local authorities were quick to respond to the emergency call, with the Wood County Sheriff’s Department arriving at the scene around 3:45 a.m. Their efforts to assess and manage the situation continued for several hours, and the scene was ultimately cleared just before 11 a.m.

The incident has left the community in mourning, as the loss of these young lives serves as a painful reminder of the importance of road safety and responsible driving. Investigations into the exact circumstances surrounding the crash are ongoing, as authorities work diligently to piece together the events leading up to this tragic accident.
