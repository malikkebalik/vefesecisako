Hilary Duff was born as Hilary Erhard Duff on September 28, 1987, in Houston, Texas, United States. Duff's birth sign is Libra. Her parents are Robert Erhard Duff and Susan Colleen, a homemaker turned film and music producer. Duff and her older sister named Haylie Duff were raised between Houston and San Antonio.

Her mother encouraged both Hilary and Haylie to enroll in acting, singing, and ballet classes. The siblings earned roles in local theater productions and later participated in a touring Ballet production of The Nutcracker. The pair also modeled for various clothing brands. In 1993, the Duff sisters and their mother moved to California. Due to her acting career, Duff was home-schooled from the age of eight.

Hilary appeared in the titular character of the Disney Channel series, Lizzie McGuire. She quickly became a teen idol and household name. Disney Company made an estimated $100 million from the show merchandise alone by marketing through soundtracks, books, dolls, toys, and video games based on Duff's character.

Hilary debuted with the 2002 feature film, Human Nature. In 2003, she appeared in the film, Agent Cody Banks with Frankie Muniz. The same year she released her second studio album, 'Metamorphosis' which went triple platinum and sell over five million copies within its first two years of release.

In 2019, she appeared in the American horror thriller film, The Haunting of Sharon Tate. In January 2019, Duff invested with Chelsea Handler for the cosmetics line Nudestix.
